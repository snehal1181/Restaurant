Since 1998

A Passion for Perfection

Founded by Chef Antoine Laurent, Golden Crown began as a small, intimate bistro with a simple philosophy: to honour the ingredients and the time-tested techniques of classical cooking while embracing modern innovation.

Over the decades, we have evolved into a destination for gastronomes from around the world. Every dish is a story told on a plate, crafted with passion, precision, and the finest seasonal produce available.

Our commitment to excellence extends beyond the kitchen. Our dining room, designed with timeless elegance, provides a sanctuary where every guest is treated as family, and every meal becomes a cherished memory.
